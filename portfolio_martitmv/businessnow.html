About

The application is designed to cater to the needs of busy professionals in the finance industry who have limited time and want to stay updated with the latest news in their field.

To achieve this, I invested time in extensive research to understand the user's needs and problems, using the Double Diamond UX model. Through this research, I developed a user persona that represents our target audience.

The user is a busy professional who needs access to multiple news sources in real-time, without having to switch between multiple websites. He values convenience and wants to bookmark important articles and share them with his colleagues with ease.

The search bar allows the user to look up anything business themed. Articles offer a detailed view, which displays the title, image, and short text/description. This feature ensures that the user can quickly scan through the articles and the ones to read. The app allows the user to easily bookmark, share and delete articles as desired.

Main goal was to create an intuitive and easy-to-use interface, guided by Apple's design guidelines, also teacher's and user's feedback.
