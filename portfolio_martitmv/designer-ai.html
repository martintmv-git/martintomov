CONCEPT

Designer AI is an innovative platform that simplifies the interior design process. To get started, users simply need to upload a photo of their space and provide a brief description as a prompt of what they're looking for. The result could then be used as inspiration for actual interior designers, or used for DIY projects that are currently on the rise.

Designer AI leverages the power of OpenAI API and DALL-E to create stunning interior design concepts for users. The process involves using a trained machine learning model that specializes in designing various types of room interiors. Once a user uploads a photo of their space and provides a brief description of what they're looking for, the trained model analyzes the image and generates a concept design that matches the user's description.

This is an open project that welcomes new ideas and contributions from the community. Collaboration and feedback are essential for creating a platform that truly meets the needs of users.
