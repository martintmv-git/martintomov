CONCEPT

e-iDBG is an app designed to store digital identity documents such as passports, identity cards and driver's licenses for Bulgaria, in a safe and easy-to-use way.

The app serves as an online identity verifier. It is designed to be a government app that enables users to identify themselves while conducting business with educational institutes, healthcare institutions, or their national tax agency.

My goal is to expand the app's capabilities, develop and integrate it with various government and private services to provide seamless digital identity management.

This is an open project that welcomes new ideas and contributions from the community. Collaboration and feedback are essential for creating a platform that truly meets the needs of users.

If you would like to contribute or know more about the project, Contact me.
