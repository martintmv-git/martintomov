About

The goal of the content management system - RedactiePortal, is to empower journalists to report the news anytime, anywhere with a modern, flexible design solution for story-based journalist workflow.

To meet the needs of our client, we needed to create a software solution that was user-friendly, customizable, and compatible with multiple platforms, including mobile and web. We also recognized the need for a web scraping feature to help our client keep up with competitor strategies and increase engagement on their website.

To achieve this, my main task was to implemented UX/UI design solutions in Figma to fulfil our client's needs. My approach was focused on research and empathy towards the journalists, ensuring that the solution fits its purpose.
