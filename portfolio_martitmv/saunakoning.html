About

Saunakoning wanted to modernize their website. To deliver a website that met their expectations, I spent a significant amount of time researching their brand and target audience. I used various tools and techniques to put myself in the shoes of their users and gain a better understanding of their needs and preferences. This helped me develop a clear vision for the new website and lay the foundation for a successful design.

I incorporated their brand's colors and style into the design, but also added a modern, futuristic touch that would set them apart from their competition.

One of the main challenges was to create a user-friendly interface that was simple enough for anyone to use, but also featured enough functionality to meet their needs. I achieved this by using a minimalist design with clear, easy-to-understand navigation, which made it easy for users to find what they were looking for.

The mobile version of the website was not prioritized during its initial announcement and development, which could result in a less-than-optimal user experience for mobile users.
