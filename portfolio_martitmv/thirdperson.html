About

Third Person is a user-centered application designed to streamline the process of previewing, editing, and creating brand style guides. Its minimalist interface, combined with its accessibility, make it an ideal solution for content creators looking to communicate a consistent brand message to their audience.

With Third Person, tracking and maintaining the design aspects of your brand is made simple. The platform allows you to edit saved information such as logos, typography, and colors, and also offers a dashboard tool to create new style guides with ease. You can name a project and upload your custom brand identity in just a few steps.

To meet the needs of a diverse range of users, Third Person is available on both mobile and web platforms. This versatility allows you to work on larger screens when necessary, while still having the option to use the app on smaller screens when the situation demands it.
