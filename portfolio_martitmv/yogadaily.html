About

Starting or resuming yoga can be daunting, especially if you're a complete beginner. While there are countless videos and group sessions available, it can be challenging to find good guidance, and many people feel too shy and insecure to attend physical sessions.

That's why I decided to develop a convenient and easy-to-use yoga progressive web app that is accessible on all screen sizes.

The heart of my yoga application is comfort and relaxation, with a focus on creating the right ambiance through carefully chosen peaceful colors. The goal is to provide users with flawless experience, which is why the design remains clean and uncluttered.

The range of features provides users with the guidance they need to practice yoga with confidence. Video explanations with voice guidance enable users to learn the basic yoga poses, while live sessions offer the opportunity to practice with an experienced teacher in real-time. For those who prefer to practice independently, the application includes a music library featuring relaxing tracks that help to create a calming environment for yoga practice.
